Quick Homemade Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup known for its rich broth, chewy noodles, and delicious toppings. This version is a simple, homemade take on ramen that’s fast, flavorful, and comforting.

Ingredients (2 Servings)

  • 2 packs instant ramen noodles (discard seasoning packets)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
  • 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • Chopped scallions and sesame seeds for garnish

Directions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pot. Sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant (1–2 minutes).
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until softened.
  3. Pour in broth, soy sauce, miso paste (if using), and chili sauce. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add ramen noodles and cook until just tender (about 3 minutes).
  5. Ladle soup and noodles into bowls. Top with boiled egg halves, scallions, and sesame seeds.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy immediately.

Note: You can add extras like spinach, corn, tofu, or shredded chicken for variety.
