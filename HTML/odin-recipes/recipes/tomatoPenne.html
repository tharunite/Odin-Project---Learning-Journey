Penne Tomato Pasta

Penne Tomato Pasta is a simple, comforting Italian dish made with tender penne pasta tossed in a fresh, rich tomato sauce. Perfect for weeknights, it's quick to make and always satisfying.

Ingredients (2–3 Servings)

  • 200g penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes, chopped (or 1 cup canned crushed tomatoes)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions

  1. Boil penne pasta in salted water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Sauté garlic and onions until translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, oregano, chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook until tomatoes break down and sauce thickens (10–15 mins).
  4. Add cooked penne pasta to the sauce and toss well to coat evenly.
  5. Simmer for 2–3 minutes, letting flavors blend.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan if desired.

Tip: For added richness, stir in a splash of cream or a pat of butter at the end.
